MOS Listening Test

Blind evaluation of speech pauses · please use headphones in a quiet room

You will hear 15 short spoken sentences in random order (about 6 minutes total). Please use headphones in a quiet room and do not look at any text while listening.

How to rate — please read carefully:
• Completely ignore the audio quality. Do not judge whether the voice sounds electronic, robotic or synthetic, or whether individual words are pronounced oddly. Many clips are computer-generated and will sound artificial — that is not what we are measuring.
• Judge only the speaker's pauses and rhythm: based on where they pause and how the speech is phrased, how clearly can you perceive the syntactic boundaries — for example where the subject (the person/thing) ends and the verb (the action) begins?
• 1 = Bad (pausing/rhythm makes the structure confusing or misleading)  →  5 = Good (pausing/rhythm makes the structure perfectly clear).
